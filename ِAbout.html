About US

Telda is a pioneering Egyptian fintech company founded in 2021 with the vision of reshaping how young people interact with money. Built for a generation that values speed, simplicity, and independence, Telda offers a digital-first financial experience that eliminates the barriers of traditional banking. At its core, Telda provides a mobile application and a payment card that allow users to send, spend, and manage money instantly, securely, and socially. Unlike conventional banks, Telda focuses on accessibility and design. Signing up requires only a national ID, and users can issue a Telda card without fees or complex paperwork. The card works globally—online, in-store, and at ATMs—while also integrating with Apple Pay for seamless contactless payments. Every transaction is accompanied by real-time notifications, ensuring transparency and control. Telda’s platform is not just about payments; it is about making money management social and intuitive. Peer-to-peer transfers are designed to feel as easy as sending a message, enriched with emojis, GIFs, and personal notes. This approach transforms financial transactions from a cold, technical process into a lively, human-centered experience. The company’s mission is to empower Millennials and Gen Z with financial freedom, giving them tools to track spending, gain insights, and build healthier financial habits. By combining technology, design, and community-driven features, Telda positions itself as more than a fintech startup—it is a lifestyle brand that redefines financial inclusion in Egypt and beyond. Telda’s long-term vision is to create a borderless financial ecosystem where money moves as freely as information, enabling young people to participate fully in the digital economy. With its innovative approach, Telda is setting the stage for a future where financial services are not only accessible but also engaging, social, and empowering.
